Get Found in AI Search

Understanding how AI search impacts your business and what you can do about it.

What is AI Visibility?

AI visibility refers to how easily AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and other AI-powered search engines can find, understand, and recommend your business to users.

When someone asks an AI tool for recommendations — like "find me a good plumber in Temecula" — the AI searches the web and provides answers based on what it finds. If your business isn't structured properly, you may not show up at all.

That's where AI visibility comes in. It's about making sure your website has the right structure, content, and signals that AI tools look for when making recommendations.

Why This Matters

Search has changed. People aren't just typing keywords into Google anymore. They're asking AI tools questions and expecting direct answers.

If your business isn't visible to these AI systems, you're missing out on potential customers who are actively looking for what you offer.

AI visibility ensures that when someone asks for recommendations in your industry and area, your business has the best chance of being suggested.
